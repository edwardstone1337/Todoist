How TaskGen works

What TaskGen is

TaskGen is a simple tool that helps you create Todoist task links quickly. These links can be used with QR codes and NFC tags to capture tasks instantly from anywhere.

What Todoist is

Todoist is a popular task management app that helps you organize and track your work. It supports natural language input, so you can type tasks like "Buy groceries tomorrow" and it understands the due date automatically.

What TaskGen links do

TaskGen creates special Todoist Quick Add links that open the Todoist app with your task pre-filled. When you tap or scan these links on a mobile device, Todoist opens with your task ready to add—including the task name, due date, priority, and project.

On desktop, these links open Todoist in your browser and send the task to your Inbox. On mobile, the task keeps your specified project when added.

What QR codes do

QR codes are scannable images that contain your Todoist task link. You can print them, display them on screens, or save them to your phone. When someone scans the QR code with their phone's camera, it opens the Todoist Quick Add link automatically.

How to use links with NFC tags

  1. Generate your Todoist task link using TaskGen
  2. Copy the generated URL
  3. Open an NFC writing app like NFC Tools on your phone
  4. Select "Write" or "Add a record"
  5. Choose "Add a URL" or "Add a web address"
  6. Paste your TaskGen link
  7. Hold your phone near the NFC tag and write the link
  8. Test by tapping the tag—it should open Todoist with your task

Example use cases

  • Place an NFC tag on your desk to quickly add tasks to a "Work" project
  • Put a QR code on your fridge to capture grocery items to a "Shopping" project
  • Use NFC tags in different rooms to route tasks to room-specific projects
  • Share QR codes with team members for collaborative task capture
  • Create recurring task links for weekly meetings or routines

Note: This is an unofficial tool and isn't affiliated with Todoist.
